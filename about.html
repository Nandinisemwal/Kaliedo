About Kaleido

Reimagining how people experience festivals

Kaleido was created to make cultural festivals easier to explore, more accessible, and much more engaging for everyone.

The Problem

Many festivals still rely on traditional programming that can limit engagement. Festival-goers often struggle with discoverability, navigation, and meaningful participation, creating passive experiences instead of memorable ones.

The Solution

Kaleido centralizes event discovery, tickets, schedules, maps, and updates into one platform. This improves accessibility, makes navigation easier, and creates a more immersive and personalized festival experience.

Summary

A more seamless and interactive festival journey

Kaleido is a platform designed to reimagine how people experience cultural festivals in Calgary. It addresses issues with discoverability, accessibility, navigation, and participation by bringing key festival tools into one cohesive and engaging experience.

By transforming fragmented festival visits into smoother and more connected journeys, Kaleido encourages stronger community connection and more meaningful participation for all users.
